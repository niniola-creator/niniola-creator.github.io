About Me

Hi! I’m Hannah Niniola, a self-taught data analyst with a background in geology. My research interests focus on environmental geochemistry, pollution studies and how anthropogenic activities affect public health. I enjoy working with environmental datasets related to contamination, resource monitoring and sustainability. Right now, I'm working on how to use data to address concerns in environmental research and sustainable development. Outside of work, I enjoy building responsive websites and reading fiction, often by African authors.
